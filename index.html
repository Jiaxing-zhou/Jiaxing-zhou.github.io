About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in Structural Engineering at Lehigh University, advised by Prof. Claudia Reis. My research focuses on computational mechanics and AI for science, developing physics-informed and data-driven methods for solving complex engineering problems such as fluid-solid interaction.

Prior to joining Lehigh, I earned my B.E. in Civil Engineering and M.S. in Geotechnical Engineering from Hunan University, where I studied seismic analysis of underground structures and infrastructure resilience.
